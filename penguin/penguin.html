Penguin

Student Design Awards

For the penguin student awards I was tasked with designing a cover for the book, A Short History of Nearly Everything by Bill Bryson. The book covers a wide range of topics about the history of the Universe. For the cover design, I used a range of the planets. Fitting everything in from the book is an impossible task so I wanted to focus on one of the elements in the book, this being the planets and our solar system. It is such a complex thing and understanding the solar system is a difficult task. Therefore, simplifying a range of the planets and using the idea of Newtons cradle to imply movement and the constant motion of the planets allowed for me to simplify the solar system down.
