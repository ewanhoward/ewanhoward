Branding and Packaging

Propel, a sports nutrition brand focusing using all natural ingredients and cutting down on litter and waste produced by energy gels and various other products. Aimed at 20-45 year olds runners/athletes. By using larger packets so less packaging is needed, therefore less waste is produced. As well as using a recycling scheme to allow for the consumer to send their waste back to us for reuse. Sports Nutrition is a wasteful industry that produces lots of plastic waste. 32% of plastic waste goes into the ocean.
I made the packaging clear and easy to understand. This started from my branding. I split the brand up into using three different colours, red, yellow and green, each relating to a different product range. The reason for doing this to make it extremely clear for my target audience to recognise different products and know what they are for and when to use them. This continued with packaging itself. I kept the three main colours running through my products.
