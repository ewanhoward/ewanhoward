North Sea Observatory

Exhibition Design

For this project, I was tasked with designing a series of posters for an exhibition at the North Sea Costal Observatory. After my initial research and ideation, I wanted to focus on litter in coastal areas. To demonstrate this clearly, I used a hand-crafted style to make these posters. I used simple materials, plastic bags and some netting. The reason for going down this route is because it the hand-crafted feel gives of more emotion and realism compared to something designed digitally. Therefore capturing the emotion and problems in coastal areas a little more.
