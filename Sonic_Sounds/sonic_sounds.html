Sonic Sounds

Information Graphic

For this project, we were first tasked with performing a musical interpretation of Mark Rothko’s painting ‘Black, Red and Black’. I first designed and make an instrument, taking features from a Harp whilst adding some unusual twists. After the performance, I took the data and had to interpret it and show this in the form as an information graphic. The colour pallet takes inspiration from the painting as well as the way the data is show using different sharpness’s to represent each instrument.
